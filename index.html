FEATURES

Why Choose Us?

At the Gents of Brooklyn Academy we have over 10 years experience in delivering barbering qualifications, and over 25 years industry experience in varied environments. We deliver our services in high end barber shops with an emphasis on customer service and client experience, always going that extra mile to create a unique visit everytime.

Master Barbers

Our master barbers and assessors are specialists in classic and contemporary barbering. Fusing original techniques and cutting styles with modern processes and tools to achieve a high end finish to every style. Our assessors experience allows us to pass on our workplace skills to the next generation through our workshops.

Expert Advice

Hints and tips are vital on your learning journey. Get direct guidance from experts in barbering, allowing you to avoid picking up bad habits and progress quicker, allowing you to maximise your investment.

Amazing Results

With our proven training process, work-ready skills are achieved in just nine weeks, allowing our learners to earn quickly.

SERVICES

What We Offer

We offer accredited NVQ qualifications in barbering at Level 2, also offering accredited micro courses in each unit, allowing our learners to tailor their learner journey to suit their individual needs.

Hair Styling

You will be taught a diverse range of contemporary and classic styling, gaining all the required knowledge

Beard Trim

You will be taught how to trim and shape longer and shorter facial hair with various growth patterns and textures

Cuts & Fades

You will cover a wide variety of cutting styles, learning the fading process on all hair types

Micro Courses Available

Our micro course can be tailored to suit your requirements and learning needs

Open Day in Penarth - Sunday 6th February

34 Windsor Road, Penarth, CF64 1JH

9.00 am - Meet & greet Introductions, coffee and refreshments.
9.30 am - Video presentation We will start things off with a brief visual and audio description of the GB Academy. What we do, our experience and philosophy.
